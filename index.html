Privacy Policy

PRIVACY POLICY
Biplanes Fight

─────────────────────────────────────────────

1. OVERVIEW

Biplanes Fight ("the App") is a biplane combat game developed as an independent
project. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is
used, and your rights regarding that information.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. The App does not collect data
and never sells or shares personal information with third
parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

─────────────────────────────────────────────

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data exclusively on your device:

• Player progress – Your current level and XP points are saved in the app's
  local storage (UserDefaults) and protected with a device-bound cryptographic
  key stored in the iOS Keychain. This data never leaves your device during
  offline (bot) play.

• Game preferences – The number of completed fights is stored locally to
  determine when to prompt you for an App Store review.

This locally stored data is never transmitted to our servers or to any third
party.

2.2 Information Transmitted During Online Play

When you use the "Play Online" feature, the following technical data is sent to
our game server to facilitate matchmaking and real-time gameplay:

• Your current player level (an integer, not linked to any personal identity).
• In-game state updates (positions, actions) required to synchronise gameplay
  between two players.

No personally identifiable information (name, email, device identifiers, IP
geolocation, etc.) is collected or stored by the server beyond what is strictly
required for the current game session.

2.3 Contact Email

If you choose to contact us via the in-app "Contact" button, your email client
will open a pre-addressed message to biplanes.fight@gmail.com. Any information
you include in that email is voluntarily provided by you and is governed by your
email provider's privacy policy. We use email replies solely to respond to your
inquiry.

─────────────────────────────────────────────

3. INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT

• We do not collect your name, email address, or any account credentials.
• We do not collect device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, or similar).
• We do not collect location data.
• We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-
  reporting SDKs.
• We do not use cookies or web tracking technologies.
• We do not share any data with third parties.

─────────────────────────────────────────────

4. APP STORE REVIEW REQUESTS

The App may ask you to rate it on the App Store after certain gameplay
milestones. This is handled entirely by Apple's SKStoreReviewController API.
No data is transmitted to us as a result of this prompt; Apple manages the
review process independently.

─────────────────────────────────────────────

5. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under
the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction).
Because we collect no personal information at all, the App is safe for users
of all ages in this regard.

─────────────────────────────────────────────

6. DATA SECURITY

Locally stored progress data is protected by iOS-native security mechanisms:
a cryptographic HMAC secret is stored in the iOS Keychain with the
kSecAttrAccessibleAfterFirstUnlockThisDeviceOnly policy, meaning it cannot be
accessed by other apps or transferred to another device.

─────────────────────────────────────────────

7. DATA RETENTION AND DELETION

All data stored by the App resides on your device. You can delete it at any
time by uninstalling the App, which removes all locally stored data including
UserDefaults entries and Keychain items associated with the App.

─────────────────────────────────────────────

8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be
reflected in an updated version distributed with a new App release. We
encourage you to review this policy periodically.

─────────────────────────────────────────────
